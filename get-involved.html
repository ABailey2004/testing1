Our Services

Blue Light RPC operates authentic Scottish emergency services, providing realistic roleplay experiences based on real-world procedures and protocols.

Police Scotland

Experience authentic Scottish policing with realistic procedures, investigations, traffic enforcement, and community policing. Work your way up through the ranks in Scotland's national police service.

Scottish Ambulance Service

Provide emergency medical care and patient transport across Scotland. Respond to 999 calls, conduct patient assessments, and work alongside other emergency services in critical situations.

Scottish Fire and Rescue Service

Fight fires, conduct technical rescues, and provide emergency response across Scotland. Engage in fire suppression, road traffic accidents, and community safety initiatives.

Civilian Roles

Play as a member of the public, creating realistic scenarios for emergency services. Experience life in Scotland from a civilian perspective, contributing to immersive roleplay scenarios.
