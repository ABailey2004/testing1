Community Rules

Guidelines for maintaining a professional and enjoyable environment

General Conduct

1. Respect and Professionalism

All members must treat each other with respect and maintain a professional attitude at all times. Harassment, discrimination, or toxic behavior will not be tolerated.

2. Age Requirement

All members must be at least 14 years of age to participate in Blue Light RPC activities.

3. Language

English is the primary language of our community. Members must be able to communicate effectively in English.

4. Real Life Priority

Real life always takes priority over roleplay. Members should never compromise their real-life responsibilities for community activities.

Voice Communication

5. Microphone Requirement

A working microphone is mandatory for all roleplay activities. Text-only communication is not permitted during active scenarios.

6. Voice Quality

Members must ensure their microphone provides clear audio quality. Excessive background noise or poor quality may result in removal from scenarios.

7. Push to Talk

Push to talk is required during all roleplay sessions to maintain communication clarity and professionalism.

Roleplay Standards

8. Realistic Roleplay

All roleplay must be realistic and follow real-world procedures and protocols as closely as possible.

9. Character Development

Members are encouraged to develop realistic characters with appropriate backgrounds and motivations for their chosen roles.

10. Breaking Character

Breaking character during active roleplay scenarios is prohibited unless in emergency situations or when directed by staff.

11. Metagaming

Using information obtained outside of roleplay (OOC) for in-character advantages is strictly forbidden.

Department Specific

12. Chain of Command

All departments operate under a strict chain of command. Members must follow orders from superior officers and respect the hierarchy.

13. Training Requirements

All members must complete required training for their department before participating in active scenarios.

14. Equipment Usage

Department equipment and resources must be used appropriately and realistically according to established procedures.

Consequences

15. Disciplinary Actions

Rule violations may result in warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent removal from the community, depending on severity.

16. Appeals Process

Members may appeal disciplinary actions through the proper channels. Contact staff for information on the appeals process.

Important Notice

These rules are subject to change at any time. Members are responsible for staying updated with the latest version. Rule updates will be announced in our Discord server.
